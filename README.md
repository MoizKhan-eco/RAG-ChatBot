# 🤖 DigiPal - Digital Safety Mentor for Kids

**DigiPal** is a friendly AI-powered digital literacy mentor designed for children ages 8-12. It teaches safe, responsible, and kind use of technology through interactive conversations.

## 🎯 Features

- **Voice Input**: Speak your questions using the built-in voice recorder
- **Kid-Friendly Responses**: Age-appropriate explanations in simple language
- **Digital Safety Topics**: Covers cyberbullying, online privacy, scams, gaming safety, and more
- **Interactive Learning**: Includes quizzes, tips, and engaging content
- **Powered by Google Gemini**: Advanced AI for accurate and helpful responses

## 📚 Topics Covered

- 🛡️ Online Safety & Privacy
- 🚫 Cyberbullying Prevention
- 🔒 Password Security & Cookies
- 🎮 Gaming Safety & Screen Time
- 📧 Recognizing Scams & Phishing
- 🌐 Digital Footprints & Ethics
- 💻 Basic Digital Skills

## 🔧 Technical Stack

- **LLM**: Google Gemini Pro
- **UI Framework**: Gradio
- **Vector Database**: ChromaDB
- **Embeddings**: Google Generative AI Embeddings
- **RAG Framework**: LangChain
- **Speech-to-Text**: Google Gemini Multimodal

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Type or Speak**: Enter your question in the text box or click "Speak" to use voice input
2. **Get Answers**: DigiPal will provide kid-friendly explanations and safety tips
3. **Learn Safely**: All responses are designed to be age-appropriate and educational

## 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 For Parents & Educators

DigiPal is designed to supplement, not replace, adult supervision. It:
- Never collects personal information
- Provides age-appropriate content only
- Encourages children to talk to trusted adults about serious concerns
- Models respectful and safe online behavior

## 📝 Privacy & Safety

- No personal data collection
- No chat history stored permanently
- Designed with child safety as top priority
- Compliant with children's online privacy standards
